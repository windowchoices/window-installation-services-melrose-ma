Our Window Services

Double-Hung Windows

Our double-hung windows offer classic style with modern functionality. Both sashes move vertically, allowing for excellent ventilation control. These windows are perfect for traditional and contemporary homes alike, providing easy cleaning and maintenance with their tilt-in feature.

Casement Windows

Casement windows open outward with a crank mechanism, providing excellent ventilation and unobstructed views. Their tight seal when closed makes them one of the most energy-efficient window options available. Ideal for hard-to-reach places and contemporary home designs.

Bay & Bow Windows

Create a dramatic focal point in any room with our custom bay and bow windows. These projecting windows not only add architectural interest but also create additional interior space. Perfect for reading nooks or displaying decorative items while flooding your room with natural light.

Sliding Windows

Our sliding windows offer a modern, space-saving solution that's perfect for contemporary homes. They glide horizontally on tracks, making them easy to operate and ideal for areas where outward-opening windows aren't practical. Great for wide wall spaces and providing panoramic views.

Picture Windows

Maximize your views with our fixed picture windows that don't open, providing an uninterrupted panoramic view of the outdoors. These windows are perfect for capturing beautiful scenery and flooding your interior with natural light while offering superior energy efficiency.

Custom Windows

Can't find exactly what you're looking for? Our custom window service allows you to design the perfect windows for your unique needs. From unusual shapes and sizes to specific performance requirements, we'll work with you to create windows that perfectly match your vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does window installation typically take?

The duration of window installation depends on several factors including the number of windows, type of windows, and the condition of your existing frames. For a standard single-family home with 10-15 windows, our professional team typically completes the installation within 1-2 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine while ensuring the highest quality installation.

What types of windows are most energy-efficient?

The most energy-efficient windows typically feature double or triple-pane glass with low-E coatings, argon gas fills, and warm-edge spacers. Casement and awning windows tend to be the most energy-efficient operating styles because they seal tightly when closed. Vinyl and fiberglass frames also offer excellent insulation properties. We can help you select the most energy-efficient options for your specific needs and local climate conditions in Melrose, MA.

Do you offer financing options for window replacement?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make your window replacement project more affordable. We partner with several reputable lenders to provide competitive rates and terms. Additionally, energy-efficient windows may qualify for local rebates or federal tax credits. Our team can provide detailed information about available financing options and help you determine the best solution for your budget.

What is the warranty on your windows and installation?

We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Our window products come with manufacturer warranties that typically range from 10 years to lifetime coverage, depending on the product. Additionally, we provide a separate warranty on our installation workmanship, ensuring that your windows are properly installed and will perform as expected. Specific warranty details will be provided with your quote.

Can you install windows in historic homes while maintaining their character?

Absolutely! We specialize in preserving the architectural integrity of historic homes while upgrading to modern, energy-efficient windows. We offer custom window solutions that match the original style, including divided lites, authentic wood grains, and traditional operating styles. Our team has extensive experience working with historic properties in the Melrose area and understands the importance of maintaining historical accuracy while improving performance.
